About This Facility

Dilley Family Residential Center is an Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) detention facility located in Dilley, TX, 78017. This facility is part of the U.S. immigration detention system and currently holds 2,000 detainees.

The facility provides detention services for individuals awaiting immigration proceedings or deportation. ICE detention facilities are operated by private contractors or local governments under contract with the federal government.
